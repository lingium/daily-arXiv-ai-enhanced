TL;DR: 本文扩展了最优传输映射崩溃点的研究，证明了对于一般凸成本函数，崩溃点与成本函数无关，仅取决于参考测度Q中点的Tukey深度。

Motivation: 先前研究（Avella-Medina & González-Sanz 2026, Passeggeri & Paindaveine 2026）发现平方欧几里得成本下最优传输映射的崩溃点等于参考测度Q中点的Tukey深度。本文旨在将此结果推广到一般凸成本函数，验证成本函数是否影响崩溃点。

Method: 通过理论分析扩展先前结果，证明对于一般凸成本函数，最优传输映射的崩溃点特性保持不变。主要采用数学推导和理论证明方法。

Result: 证明了成本函数对最优传输映射的崩溃点没有影响，崩溃点完全由参考测度Q中点的Tukey深度决定。这一结果为广泛的正则成本函数类提供了统一的崩溃点特性。

Conclusion: 本文给出了最优传输映射崩溃点的最终特征化：对于一般凸成本函数，崩溃点与成本函数无关，仅取决于参考测度中点的Tukey深度。这表明所有基于传输的分位数都具有相同的高崩溃点特性。

Abstract: Two recent works, Avella-Medina and González-Sanz (2026) and Passeggeri and Paindaveine (2026), studied the robustness of the optimal transport map through its breakdown point, i.e., the smallest fraction of contamination that can make the map take arbitrarily aberrant values. Their main finding is the following: let $P$ and $Q$ denote the target and reference measures, respectively, and let $T$ be the optimal transport map for the squared Euclidean cost. Then, the breakdown point of $T(u)$, when $P$ is perturbed and $Q$ is fixed, coincides with the Tukey depth of $u$ relative to $Q$. In this note, we extend this result to general convex cost functions, demonstrating that the cost function does not have any impact on the breakdown point of the optimal transport map. Our contribution provides a definitive characterization of the breakdown point of the optimal transport map. In particular, it shows that for a broad class of regular cost functions, all transport-based quantiles enjoy the same high breakdown point properties.

### [2] [Equivalence testing with data-dependent and post-hoc equivalence margins](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16213)
*Stan Koobs,Nick W. Koning*

Main category: math.ST

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统等价性检验需要预先指定一个固定的等价边界$Δ$，但在实践中这个边界往往缺乏客观依据。作者认为需要一种更实用的替代方法来解决这个长期存在的问题。

Method: 基于e值构建数据依赖的边界$\widehatΔ_α$，该边界能以$1-α$概率覆盖真实效应$μ$。方法适用于严格全正三阶模型，包含经典的z检验和t检验设置，并可推广到均匀有效的边界曲线。

Result: 提出了一种新的等价性检验范式，通过数据依赖的边界$\widehatΔ_α$提供比传统固定边界检验更强的决策保证。该方法在严格全正三阶模型下有效，包含经典检验设置。

Conclusion: 数据依赖的边界$\widehatΔ_α$比固定边界$Δ$的检验结果对决策者更有用，提供了更强的统计保证。这为等价性检验建立了一个新的理论基础。

Abstract: Equivalence testing compares the hypothesis that an effect $μ$ is large against the alternative that it is negligible. Here, `large' is classically expressed as being larger than some `equivalence margin' $Δ$. A longstanding problem is that this margin must be specified but can rarely be objectively justified in practice. We lay the foundation for an alternative paradigm, arguing to instead report a data-dependent margin $\widehatΔ_α$ that bounds the true effect $μ$ with probability $1 - α$. Our key argument is that $\widehatΔ_α$ is more useful than a test outcome at a fixed margin $Δ$, as measured by the guarantees it offers to decision makers. We generalize this to a curve of margins $α\mapsto \widehatΔ_α$, uniformly valid under the post-hoc selection of the margin. These ideas rely on e-values, which we derive for models that are strictly totally positive of order 3, nesting the classical z-test and t-test settings.

### [4] [High-dimensional estimation with missing data: Statistical and computational limits](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16712)
*Kabir Aladin Verchand,Ankit Pensia,Saminul Haque,Rohith Kuditipudi*

Main category: math.ST

TL;DR: 该论文研究了在缺失数据下高效计算估计的问题，特别关注可实现的污染模型。对于高斯数据，在均值估计和协方差估计中发现了统计-计算差距，但在线性回归中这种差距消失。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究在缺失数据下高效计算估计的问题，特别是当观测数据受到缺失非随机（MNAR）机制污染时。需要理解在这种复杂缺失机制下，计算效率与统计性能之间的权衡关系。

Method: 采用可实现的污染模型，其中ε比例的观测受到任意未知MNAR机制影响。对于高斯数据，分析均值估计（ℓ₂范数）和协方差估计（相对算子范数）的样本复杂度。使用计算不可行算法作为基准，对比多项式时间算法（如平方和算法）的性能。

Result: 1. 均值估计：计算不可行算法需要n≳de^{1/ρ²}样本，而多项式时间算法需要n≳d^{1/ρ²}样本，存在统计-计算差距。2. 协方差估计：类似差距存在。3. 线性回归：没有这种差距，简单的强凸经验风险最小化在多项式时间内几乎达到信息理论下界。

Conclusion: 在缺失数据下的估计问题中，均值估计和协方差估计存在统计-计算差距，表明计算效率与统计精度之间存在根本性权衡。然而在线性回归中，这种差距消失，简单的多项式时间算法就能达到接近最优性能。

Abstract: We consider computationally-efficient estimation of population parameters when observations are subject to missing data. In particular, we consider estimation under the realizable contamination model of missing data in which an $ε$ fraction of the observations are subject to an arbitrary (and unknown) missing not at random (MNAR) mechanism. When the true data is Gaussian, we provide evidence towards statistical-computational gaps in several problems. For mean estimation in $\ell_2$ norm, we show that in order to obtain error at most $ρ$, for any constant contamination $ε\in (0, 1)$, (roughly) $n \gtrsim d e^{1/ρ^2}$ samples are necessary and that there is a computationally-inefficient algorithm which achieves this error. On the other hand, we show that any computationally-efficient method within certain popular families of algorithms requires a much larger sample complexity of (roughly) $n \gtrsim d^{1/ρ^2}$ and that there exists a polynomial time algorithm based on sum-of-squares which (nearly) achieves this lower bound. For covariance estimation in relative operator norm, we show that a parallel development holds. Finally, we turn to linear regression with missing observations and show that such a gap does not persist. Indeed, in this setting we show that minimizing a simple, strongly convex empirical risk nearly achieves the information-theoretic lower bound in polynomial time.
