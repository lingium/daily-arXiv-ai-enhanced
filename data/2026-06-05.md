### [1] [Weighting a Census as a Non-Probability Sample: A Doubly Robust Framework for Correcting Differential Undercoverage in Uruguay's 2023 Census](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05666)
*Ferreira Juan Pablo,Goyeneche Juan Jose*

Main category: stat.ME

TL;DR: 本文针对2023年乌拉圭人口普查中10.3%的漏报率及非随机缺失问题，提出了一种基于双重稳健估计量的加权方法，结合网络链接率作为联系代理的分段响应倾向模型与普查人口统计总量的校准，有效修正了因覆盖不足导致的选择性偏差，并在三百万记录规模上验证了其对社会指标的影响。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 乌拉圭2023年人口普查漏报率高达10.3%，且漏报集中在脆弱地区、农村及年轻成年人中，呈现非随机特征。虽然整合行政记录恢复了汇总计数，但无法解决结果变量的选择性偏差（因行政记录缺少核心普查变量、存在城乡和制度可见性偏差、无法重建家庭结构）。仅依赖已普查微观数据的估计仍然存在偏差，亟需有效方法修正此类非随机缺失问题。

Method: 将有效普查家庭视为非概率样本（选择机制未知），采用双重稳健估计量构建权重：①建立分段响应倾向模型，将网络链接率作为联系代理；②结合性别、年龄、部门等普查人口统计总量进行校准。双重稳健估计量在倾向模型或校准模型中任一正确指定时均可获得一致估计，从而对覆盖不足的误设具有鲁棒性。

Result: 在三百万记录规模上应用该方法，记录了其对各项社会指标的影响，并基于等效分层整群设计提出了方差近似方法。

Conclusion: 建立了方法论框架，指导各国统计机构根据其现有登记数据和辅助数据优化无回答调整策略。

Abstract: The 2023 Uruguayan Census recorded a population of 3,444,451 with an estimated undercoverage of 10.3%. Post-enumeration evidence shows that omission was non-random, concentrated in vulnerable areas, rural territories, and among young adults. Integrating administrative records (AR) recovered aggregate counts but did not resolve selection bias in outcome variables, as AR lack core census variables, exhibit urbanicity and institutional-visibility biases, and do not reconstruct households. Estimates derived from enumerated microdata remain biased. We treat effectively enumerated households as a non-probability sample with an unknown selection mechanism and construct weights using a doubly robust (DR) estimator. This framework combines a segment-level response-propensity model, using the web linkage rate as a contact proxy, with calibration to combined-census demographic totals (sex, age, department). Because the DR estimator is consistent when either model is correctly specified, it provides robustness against undercoverage misspecification. We describe the application at a scale of three million records, document its effect on social indicators, and present a variance approximation based on an equivalent stratified cluster design. Finally, we establish a methodological framework to guide national statistical offices on optimizing non-response adjustments based on their available registers and paradata.

### [2] [Trace-Class Results for MCMC Algorithms for Student-t Regression Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05672)
*Yasuyuki Hamura*

Main category: math.ST

TL;DR: 本文研究了学生-t回归模型中MCMC算法的可跟踪性，发现标准数据增强算法在无信息先验下不可跟踪，而折叠Gibbs算法可跟踪；在正态-逆伽马先验下，标准算法可跟踪。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 评估不同MCMC算法在学生-t回归模型中的效率，特别是马尔可夫链是否具有可跟踪性（trace-class性质），以指导算法选择。

Method: 对比两种MCMC算法：标准数据增强算法（数据增强）与折叠Gibbs算法（collapsed Gibbs），分别在两种先验设定（无信息先验、正态-逆伽马先验）下分析马尔可夫算子的可跟踪性。

Result: 无信息先验下：标准数据增强算法的马尔可夫算子不可跟踪，折叠Gibbs算法可跟踪；正态-逆伽马先验下：标准数据增强算法可跟踪。

Conclusion: 可跟踪性（trace-class）依赖于先验分布与算法选择；折叠Gibbs算法在无信息先验下更具理论优势，为标准数据增强算法提供了更优替代。

Abstract: In this paper, we consider MCMC algorithms for Student-$t$ regression models. We investigate the efficiency of Markov chains based on the algorithms in terms of whether trace-class results hold or not. We first consider the case where the regression coefficients and error variance follow the invariant improper prior distributions. The Markov operator associated with a standard data augmentation algorithm is not trace-class but that associated with a collpased Gibbs algorithm is trace-class. We next consider the case where the parameters follow a normal-inverse gamma distribution. In this case, the standard Markov operator is trace-class.

</details>

### [3] [Finding Most Influential Sets](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05919)
*Lucas D. Konrad,Nikolas Kuschnig*

Main category: stat.ML

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种高效算法，将识别最具影响力子集（MIS）的问题从组合搜索简化为单参数序列的top-k问题，实现了线性分式留出集效应下的全局最优解，且计算复杂度为O(n)。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统MIS选择需要搜索C(n,k)个子集，计算上不可行。作者旨在开发一种高效方法，使得在具有线性分式留出集效应的估计量下，能够快速识别最具影响力的k元子集。

Method: 利用Dinkelbach方法将MIS选择转化为单参数序列的top-k问题，每次迭代O(n)复杂度且有限终止。对于固定残差化输入，返回全局最优集；对于估计的 nuisance 函数，利用均匀分母和生成得分稳定性来近似一阶正交得分目标。

Result: 算法能够恢复之前计算上无法访问的精确MIS，仿真和应用验证了方法的有效性。

Conclusion: 提出的方法显著降低了MIS选择的计算复杂性，使得原本不可行的精确识别变为可能，在分离条件下可实现精确集合恢复。

Abstract: Identifying most influential sets (MIS) - size-$k$ subsets whose removal maximally changes a target estimand - is typically infeasible because it requires searching over $\binom{n}{k}$ subsets. For estimands with linear-fractional leave-set-out effects, we show that MIS selection reduces to a one-parameter sequence of top-$k$ problems. Dinkelbach's method yields an algorithm with $\mathcal{O}(n)$ cost per iteration and finite termination. For fixed residualized inputs, the algorithm returns a globally optimal set for the univariate ratio objective, including the oracle-residualized partial linear model. With estimated nuisance functions, uniform denominator and generated-score stability imply approximation to the first-order oracle orthogonal-score objective; exact set recovery follows under a separation condition. Simulations and applications show that the method recovers exact MIS that were previously computationally inaccessible.

</details>
