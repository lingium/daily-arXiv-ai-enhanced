### [1] [Anytime-Valid Federated Conformal RAG for LLM Swarms](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29139)
*Prasanjit Dubey,Xiaoming Huo*

Main category: stat.ML

TL;DR: 本文提出 Anytime-FC-RAG，将联邦共形检索增强生成从固定视野扩展到任意有效时序覆盖，保证任意停止时刻的统计有效性，并通过截断投注 e-过程实现可控警报与带宽节约。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有 FC-RAG 仅保证固定视野下的边际覆盖，而实际部署中需要在任意停止时刻（如模型刷新、带宽调整时）都能保证统计有效性；纳式组合因违反 Ville 不等式而失败。

Method: 构建逐步可累积的校准偏差预算，将边际界转化为校准好事件上的严格条件界；设计截断投注 e-过程，使其在全概率空间上为非负鞅；结合两者得到四个时序统一保证。

Result: 在 GPT-2-small + MiniLM 集群上的 MMLU、DBpedia、AG News 实验验证了预期警报率、检测延迟、包络覆盖率，并实现 14%-57% 的带宽节省。

Conclusion: Anytime-FC-RAG 在无额外假设下实现了任意有效时序覆盖，支持自适应控制并显著降低通信成本。

Abstract: Federated Conformal RAG (FC-RAG) provides distribution-free coverage for a bandwidth-limited swarm of weak language models, but only at a fixed horizon. We extend it to anytime-valid sequential coverage: validity at every stopping time, preserved under predictable adaptive control (recalibration, per-node bandwidth escalation, distilled-student refresh), at no extra cost in assumptions over fixed-horizon FC-RAG. Naive composition fails because FC-RAG's marginal coverage bound makes the betting e-process a non-supermartingale on adverse calibration draws, and Ville's inequality cannot be invoked. We give Anytime-FC-RAG, a sequential extension built on a summable per-step calibration-deviation budget that converts the marginal bound into a strict conditional bound on a calibration-good event, paired with a truncated betting e-process that is a nonnegative supermartingale on the entire probability space. From these two ingredients, we obtain four guarantees: time-uniform alarm validity $\mathbb{P}(\sup_t E_t \ge 1/δ_e) \le δ_e + δ_{\mathrm{cal}}$, a Hoeffding-stitched cumulative-miscoverage envelope at the same total budget, safety under any predictable controller (recalibration, bandwidth escalation, student refresh), and training-side error propagation across an unbounded sequence of Federated Probe-Logit Distillation (FPLD) refreshes via a summable training budget. As a practical consequence, an adaptive controller that escalates retrieval bandwidth only when the e-process crosses a warning threshold matches the alarm rate of a fixed-high-bandwidth schedule at substantially lower communication cost. Experiments on a GPT-2-small + MiniLM swarm across MMLU, DBpedia, and AG News verify the predicted alarm rate, detection delay, envelope coverage, and $14$-$57\%$ bandwidth savings; the alarm fires when and only when coverage genuinely breaks.

### [2] [A Latent Variable Model for Response Times with Individual-Specific Change-Points](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29182)
*Gabriel Wallin,Nivedita Bhaktha*

Main category: stat.ME

TL;DR: 本文提出一种包含个体变化点的对数反应时间潜变量模型，扩展了对数正态反应时间模型，允许在未观测变化点后出现项目特定的均值偏移，并通过边际最大似然估计进行参数推断。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 计算机化评估中收集的反应时间能够反映潜在反应过程，但在测试过程中可能存在个体内波动。现有模型未能充分考虑这种个体内部行为变化，需要一种统一的方法来建模反应时间中的人内变化。

Method: 提出扩展的对数正态反应时间模型，包含个体特定的离散潜变量变化点，变化点分布被建模为潜在速度的函数，采用边际最大似然估计进行参数估计，并通过模拟研究考察参数恢复和变化点估计表现。

Result: 模拟研究结果表明，在不同的边界条件、变化者比例、样本量和测试长度下，项目和结构参数均能得到准确恢复，变化点位置也能通过后验分布进行不确定性量化。

Conclusion: 该模型为建模反应时间中的人内行为变化提供了一个统一框架，支持个体水平的变化点不确定性量化和变化点效应参数的统计推断。

Abstract: Response times collected in computerised assessments provide information about the underlying response process and may exhibit within-person variation over the course of a test. We propose a latent variable model for log response times that incorporates individual-specific change-points. The model extends the log-normal response time model by allowing an item-specific shift in the mean structure after an unobserved change-point. The change-point is treated as a discrete latent variable, and its distribution is modeled as a function of latent speed. Estimation is carried out using marginal maximum likelihood. The framework yields posterior distributions for change-point locations, allowing uncertainty to be quantified at the individual level, and supports statistical inference for the change-point effect parameters. A simulation study examines parameter recovery and change-point estimation under varying boundary conditions, prevalence of changers, sample sizes, and test lengths. The results show accurate recovery of item and structural parameters. The proposed model provides a unified approach to modeling response times with within-person changes in behaviour.

### [3] [The Topological Stability Index: A Variance-Based Measure for Persistence Barcodes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29839)
*Joris Kirchner,Ioannis Diamantis*

Main category: math.ST

TL;DR: 本文提出拓扑稳定性指标（TSI），一种基于方差的持久性条形码度量，用于量化持久性寿命的离散程度，并引入其归一化版本cvTSI，建立与Rényi熵的显式代数联系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有持久熵仅依赖归一化权重，无法捕捉绝对变异性且对异质特征尺度不敏感，因此需要一种能反映绝对变异性的方差基度量。

Method: 定义TSI为持久性寿命的方差基标量度量，研究其缩放行为、平移不变性及插入/删除更新公式；引入归一化版本cvTSI，推导其与二阶Rényi熵（碰撞概率）的显式代数关系。

Result: 数值实验表明TSI对确定性趋势相对不敏感，但能强烈响应随机波动和持久性幅度的变化，捕获熵无法反映的结构变异性。

Conclusion: TSI及其归一化版本为拓扑数据分析提供了互补于熵的方差基摘要，建立了方差基与熵基度量之间的直接理论联系。

Abstract: We introduce the \emph{Topological Stability Index} (TSI), a variance-based scalar measure for persistence barcodes that quantifies the dispersion of persistence lifetimes. Unlike persistent entropy, which depends only on normalized weights, the TSI captures absolute variability and is sensitive to heterogeneous feature scales. We establish fundamental properties of the TSI, including its scaling behavior, invariance under lifetime translation and explicit update formulas under insertion and deletion of bars. We also consider a complementary first-moment-type quantity, the Topological Signal Index (TSigI), which captures the typical scale of persistence lifetimes and provides additional interpretability alongside the TSI. We further introduce a normalized version, $cv\text{TSI}$, which is scale invariant and admits an explicit algebraic relation to the Rényi entropy of order two. In particular, $cv\text{TSI}$ is an affine function of the collision probability $\sum_i p_i^2$, and therefore a monotone reparametrization of the Rényi entropy, providing a direct link between variance-based and entropy-based summaries in topological data analysis. Numerical experiments on synthetic data and stochastic time series demonstrate that the TSI captures structural variability complementary to entropy: it is relatively insensitive to deterministic trends, while responding strongly to stochastic fluctuations and variations in persistence magnitude.

</details>


### [4] [On multiplicative bias correction in kernel density estimation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30071)
*M. C. Jones,D. F. Signorini,Nils Lid Hjort*

Main category: math.ST

TL;DR: 本文结合了两种半参数密度估计方法，理论上实现了高阶偏差和更好的参数模型适应性，但模拟显示小样本下实际效果有限。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将Hjort & Glad (1995)的半参数密度估计方法（当参数车辆分布拟合数据时表现优异）与Jones, Linton & Nielsen (1995)的方法（对所有光滑密度具有高阶偏差）相结合，以同时获得两者的优势。

Method: 将两种半参数密度估计方法进行组合，提出一种新的估计器，旨在同时实现高阶偏差性质和更好的参数模型适应性。

Result: 理论上新估计器达到了高阶偏差与参数车辆模型优越性能的期望特性，但模拟表明在中小样本量下，其实际表现仅实现了理论潜力的一小部分。

Conclusion: 新方法在理论上有显著优势，但在小到中等样本量的实际应用中性能提升有限。

Abstract: Hjort and Glad (1995) present a method for semiparametric density estimation. Relative to the ordinary kernel density estimator, this technique performs much better when a parametric vehicle distribution fits the data, and otherwise performs at broadly the same level. Jones, Linton, and Nielsen (1995) present a somewhat similar method for density estimation which has higher order bias for all sufficiently smooth densities. In this paper, we combine the two methods. We show that, theoretically, the desired properties of general higher order bias allied with even better performance for an appropriate vehicle model are achieved. Simulations suggest that the new estimator realises only a little of its theoretical potential in practice for small to moderately large sample sizes.

</details>
