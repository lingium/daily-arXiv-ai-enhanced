### [1] [Statistical Significance Revisited](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06568)
*Reason Machete*

Main category: stat.OT

TL;DR: 本文回顾了显著性检验的发展历程，并系统审视了近年来对该方法的改革呼声，分析了其优缺点。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 针对显著性检验中p值阈值（如0.05）的机械使用、零假设与备择假设的二元对立等问题，学界出现了多种改革呼声，本文旨在系统审视这些呼声的合理性与局限性。

Method: 采用文献回顾与批判性分析的方法，对显著性检验的历史发展、核心争议以及近年来的改革建议（如放弃显著性检验、改用置信区间、贝叶斯决策等）进行梳理和评估。

Result: 揭示了显著性检验改革呼声中的优势（如对机械使用p值的反思、推动方法多元化）与缺陷（如某些替代方案自身的局限性、改革可能带来的新问题）。

Conclusion: 显著性检验虽有争议，但完全放弃并非最佳选择；需要更审慎地理解和使用显著性检验，同时结合其他统计方法，而非简单替换。

Abstract: Since its introduction by Fisher, the method of hypothesis testing that relies on computing error probabilities has witnessed several developments. Perhaps the most significant development was the seminal contributions of Neyman and Pearson who brought in the concept of the alternative hypothesis with its corresponding error of the second kind. Significance tests have played a major role in various scientific and technological developments, but not without controversies. Although originally cast as frequentist approaches, Bayesian ideas have been incorporated into significance tests, widening access to them. The quantities central to computations of error probabilities are the sampling distributions, which can be computed even without thresholds or alternative hypotheses. Even though Fisher used the significance threshold of 0.05 in his calculations, he cautioned against prescribing any specific threshold. Recently, there have been calls for reformation in practice with regard to the almost standard use of the significance threshold of 0.05, prepublication confirmatory studies, the dichotomous consideration of the null and alternative hypothesis and abandoning significance tests altogether in favour of other approaches such as confidence intervals and Bayesian decision theory. In this paper, we examine these calls for reform and unearth their strengths and short comings.
